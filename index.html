Gene Gray

Founder of Club Alias Records.

Gene Gray is a hip hop/pop artist from Prince George's County Maryland. He is striving to inspire other aspiring rap artists to step out and explore other lanes within the hip hop genre. He makes music that fuses the rhythms, samples, and production techniques of hip hop and dance-pop with the urban contemporary sound of R&B/melodic flows. Hoping to tap into the minds of others with his music, Gene considers himself to be a musical visionary, always looking to create a different style and sound.
